Description

This bacon-studded "cheese corn" is a popular bar snack in Korea, made of pure bacon-y, creamy, cheesy, corny decadence.

The original recipe uses a combination of mayonnaise and sweetened condensed milk, but we're going with heavy cream here for a lighter approach. This may be the first time in my career that I've "lightened up" a recipe by adding a cup of heavy cream. Garnished with green onions, this would be amazing with a cold beer!
